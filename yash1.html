Electronics and Communication Engineering Department

Vision

Electronics and Communication Engineering department shall strive to act as a podium for the development and transfer of technical competence in academics, entrepreneurship and research in the fields of EC Engineering to meet the changing needs of society.

Mission

Committed to impart excellent technical education to the students to create techno excellent citizens who will be retaining rich cultural identity to the full satisfaction of our stake holders and society at large with further commitment to serve the nation.

About Electronics and Communication

The aim of Electronics making the life of Humans more comfortable and Communications bring them together.

ECE take care of research, design, develop and test of large variety of equipment and systems.

This course has been designed to meet the needs of the modern Communications and Electronics Industry and has particular strengths in the areas of Digital Signal Processing, VLSI Design, Radio, Radar and Microwave, Antenna, Electro and Magnetic Structures.
